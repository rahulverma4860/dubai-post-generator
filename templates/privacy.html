We collect your email address and password (encrypted) when you create an account. We collect property information you enter to generate content. We collect records of content generated through your account.

Your data is stored securely on Neon PostgreSQL servers. Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
